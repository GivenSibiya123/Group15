The Educator

        About Edward

       

            Mr. Edward van Niekerk serves as the Department Head of Business Science at the Pretoria Campus.             With a career built on structure, clarity, and a genuine passion for student success, he has shaped             the academic journey of countless graduates in the business sciences.

            His teaching philosophy centres on interactive engagement             — a belief that questions asked and answered in the classroom are the most powerful learning tools             available. He is perceptive, methodical, and quietly observant in ways that consistently catch             students off guard.

            Preferring his MacBook and Safari, he navigates the digital academic space with the same precision             he brings to his teaching: minimal, purposeful, nothing busy.

Curriculum

        Modules Taught

       

WPR161

Word Processing

            Foundational digital literacy in professional document creation. Students master structured             formatting, production workflows, and the communication standards expected in modern business             environments.

ACW181

Academic Writing

            A deep dive into the art of clear, rigorous academic expression. From argumentative structure to             proper citation — students learn to communicate with precision and intellectual integrity.

ETH271

Ethics

            Exploring the moral frameworks that underpin business decisions and professional conduct. Students             examine real-world dilemmas through structured ethical reasoning and reflective discussion.
